Mother Truss Rigging System

Mother Truss System

Rigger.co.uk have installed a high specification mother truss system. This system is fully load monitored via a Kinesys Digihoist system and can be raised / lowered to any height in minutes. It has been designed to enable tenants to install the majority of their rigging with no need for work at height or powered access.

Third parties are not permitted to make attachments to the roof structure or mother truss system, this work must be undertaken by rigger.co.uk.

Rigger.co.uk will assist partially or wholly any rigging planning including weight calculations and CAD plans throughout the design phase and duration of tenancy.

For any further information please email: contact@rigger.co.uk.
